Platinum

Platinum is a premium cinema hall that allows viewers to experience maximum comfort and enjoy the highest picture quality. Functional differences and advantages of the hall:

PERSONAL SERVICE

Personal service - a button located on the seat panel allows the viewer to order food and drinks by calling the attendants.

COMFORT

Italian armchairs - offers viewers the opportunity to watch a movie in a hall with luxurious leather Italian chairs of superior comfort and a reclining back.

USB CHARGE

USB Charge - While watching a movie, you can charge your phone using special USB equipment that is installed on the seats.

Wifi

Wifi - Some viewers of the Platinum Hall cannot forget about work even during a film screening. They have high speed internet.
